About 소개

I believe in the accountability of us adults in making our students ask better questions; in convincing them that some criticisms are indeed constructive; in providing them the adequate introduction into the public sphere; in, ultimately, engaging them with the world that will be theirs to protect.
My methods include, but are not limited to, critical reasoning, essay writing, designing presentations, all the while embracing technological advancements relevant to our end goal.
